Aari Collections

Aari work is a type of Indian embroidery that involves intricate thread work and a variety of stitches and materials.

  • Materials
Aari work uses a hooked needle called an aari, as well as colorful beads, stones, zari, and gold or silver thread.
  • Stitches
    • Aari work uses a variety of stitches, including chain, backstitch, satin, Kutch, Zardosi, and beadwork.
  • Design
    • Aari work often features motifs inspired by nature, such as leaves, flowers, trees, birds, and animals.
  • Process
    • Aari work involves stretching fabric over a frame, tracing a design, and then embroidering the design with the thread.
  • Products
    • Aari work is used to decorate a variety of items, including blouses, dresses, sarees, churidars, bedcovers, cushion covers, purses, handbags, and wall hangings.
